Services

The services of NG WAVE are to provide viable solutions to critical network issues, introduce latest features and functionalities, plan & integrate capacity expansions, optimize and improve key performance indicators, and enhance the overall quality of service of the network. Through innovation & diligence NG WAVE enables communication service providers to run their networks more efficiently and profitably, while delivering a better quality of service to their customers. 

With the help of advanced analytics, NG WAVE solutions provide actionable and measureable insights into network and customer behavior. This includes the network planning, optimization, and delivery of new and existing services, such as UMTS, LTE & 5G. Our commitment to network efficiency and quality makes NG WAVE the obvious choice for CSPs looking to optimize the QoS of their network & offer new services such as VoLTE, 5G & IoT to their subscribers.
